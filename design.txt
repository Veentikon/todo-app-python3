Design and development of to-do-app

This document outlines designing and development process of the application, it also describes chellenges and solutions
to those challenges. It also points out the concepts, ideas, anything useful learned in the process of developing this application.


General design direction
    1. Make it as modular as possible, taking "single responsibility" as the main directive in the development process
    2. Adhear to the best practices and naming conventions
    3. Most importantly, be curious and learn since the reason for this app is 1 learning and 2 solving my own problems
        with my own software solutions
